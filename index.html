Kazi Nazrul Islam

The National Poet of Bangladesh

Rebel Poet

Kazi Nazrul Islam is the National poet of Bangladesh who is especially recognized as aBidrohi Kobi(rebel poet) around the country.

Awesome Writer

Nazrul's writing explored themes such as love, freedom, humanity, and revolution. He opposed all forms of bigotry and fundamentalism, including religious, caste-based and gender-based. Throughout his career, Nazul wrote short stories, novels, and essays but is best known for his songs and poems.

"Love has no meaning or amount." -Kazil Nazrul Islam
